Tennis AI Coach

Swift · Vision · VNHumanBodyPoseObservation · On-device ML

An on-device computer vision app that analyzes tennis serves in real time. Set your phone courtside; the app detects body keypoints, finds the peak wrist frame, estimates serve speed, and generates concise, pro-style tips—without leaving the device.

Demo: on-device analysis, instant coach feedback loop.

What it does

  • Tracks pose and detects the exact frame where the wrist reaches its apex during the toss/swing.
  • Computes serve speed from video timing and camera parameters; overlays results on device.
  • Generates coaching tips based on pose dynamics (arm extension, shoulder tilt, knee drive).
  • Runs fully on-device for low latency and privacy; no upload required.

Tech highlights

  • Apple Vision framework pose estimation with temporal smoothing for robust maxima detection.
  • Frame-accurate cueing and overlay pipeline written in Swift/UIKit + SwiftUI hosting.
  • Lightweight feature extraction → rules/ML prompts to suggest actionable technique fixes.
